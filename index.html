About Me

Erik performing at the Cubby Bear in Chicago

My name is Erik, but you probably know that already. I live in the suburbs of Chicago with my lovely wife, Eunice. We like to go skiing, bowling, roller blading, and enjoy gaming. We have a cat named Spock who likes to be the center of attention. He is always looking for pets or food.


I have managed to keep myself busy during COVID-19. I spend my free time recording demos and rehearsing for my musical projects. I also exercise almost daily. Before the pandemic, I was going out to see and perform live music. Recently, I entered into the Northwestern Coding Bootcamp. It is my hope to change careers to something that is more based in creative problem solving.
